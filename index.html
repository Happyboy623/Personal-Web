About Me

I am open-minded, dedicated and adjust to changes with ease. I work on developing my professional Skills and learn new things all the time, with good communication skills, problem solving and team work. Currently I'm a Volunteer as Project Coordinator at Cape Town Fountain of Hope.

I stepped into the Tech industry without any IT background or qualifications. The first oppotunity I got to learn Software Development was when I became part of the ICT Bootcamp at CodeTelligent located in the informal settlement Township called Khayelitsha, and I dedicated myself & became committed to the program so that I can archieve my first step in Software Development. It wasn't easy at the beginning of it all, and it wasn't easy to even use a Computer properly and that was really challenging for me as a Beginer. But even though it wasn't that easy, I managed to pulled through and keep on learning as much as I could.

Just completed Systems Developement NQF Level 5 Learnership at CapaCiTi, also Worked as a Front-End Web Developer Intern at Younglings Africa. Still even toady I continue to upskill myself to improve my professional and technical skills.

Before stepping into the Tech industry, I have been serving as a Community Caregiver at Cape Town Fountain of Hope Church. My role as a Caregiver is to serve the vulnerable in the very poor communities (Mostly townships & villages) were the economy is very low. Served in Khunotswane village at the North West province as a Project supervisor, and Currently serving in the Township called Khayelitsha as a Caregiver and also as a Vertical Farming Trainer.
