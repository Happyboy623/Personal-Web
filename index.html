About Me

I'm an experienced Full Stack Developer with advance skills of Frontend & Backend development, I have 4 years of experience as a Full Stack Developer.

My Tech journey officially started the beginning of year 2020, when I joined the Software Development bootcamp at CodeTelligence, with very little basic computer skills. Before joining CodeTelligence, I firstly volunteered as a Technical Assistant at KidsInICT year 2019, and that triggered me and I started becoming curious about Programming, then I joined CodeTelligence. After getting such opportunity, I started taking advantage of online platforms such as: Udemy, Pirple, FreeCodeCamp etc. to learn and increase the level of my skills. Through the bootcamp and self-teaching, I obtained skills in Software Development and that led me to gain working experience in the following organisations:-

September 2020 I got a 12 months contract Internship role as a Frontend Web Developer at Younglings Africa.
September 2021 I got a 6 months contract with CapaCiTi as a Systems Developer candidate.
February 2023 I became part of the Youth Empowerment Programme (YEP) at PWC.

Currently, I'm running my own growing business that assists small businesses and various organizations with digital exposure in the communities that lacks accessibility to digital tools.
My Mission is to offer relevant assistance to the growing businesses using Tech Tools to improve businesses Digital performance, more especially in the villages and townships. And also I serve as a Leader and Systems Administrator volunteer at Cape Town Fountain of Hope. Cape Town Fountain of Hope is a community church that is based in the community of eNkanini in Khayelitsha, it serves as a place that offers a support to the community and it cares for the vulnerable.

The Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) conceptualizes rapid change to technology, industries and societal patterns in the 21st century due to increasing interconnectivity and smart automation, and that left a huge gab in the villages and townships economy. In this digital age, many SME's in the villages and townships are struggling to adjust doing business in this current age due to not being exposed to digital tools that they can utelize to run their businesses effectively.
My business is here to assist established small businesses in the under-accessed communities with:-
