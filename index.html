About Me

I'm an experienced Frontend Web Developer with basic knowledge of the Backend development, I have 3 years of experience as Web Developer and Web Designer. I work on developing my professional skills and learn new things all the time, my specialities include learning new skills and programming languages so that I can reach my goal of becoming a Full Stack Developer, Data Scientist, Data Analyst & Business Analyst. I have a cheerful character, very easy to work with people, and I have excellent communication & leadership skills.

I'm from underprivileged community, and I didn't have any luck to study further after completing my Matric. My Tech journey started year 2020, when I joined the Software Development bootcamp at CodeTelligence without any computer skills and IT background or qualification. CodeTelligence is a NPC(Non Profit Company) that open its doors for the underprivileged unemployed youth (like myself) to learn Software Development and equip them with ICT & Digital skills. After getting such opportunity, I became very curious to a point I started also to learn on the online platforms such as: Udemy, Pirple, FreeCodeCamp etc. With the amount of Software Development knowledge I attained at the Bootcamp and through online platforms, I managed to gain some working experience.

September 2020 I got a 12 months contract Internship role as a Frontend Web Developer at Younglings Africa. September 2021 I got a 6 months contract with CapaCiTi as a Systems Developer candidate.

Currently, I'm designing and developing websites for people with small businesses and also churches that need websites, while also looking for job opportunities. I'm also running a small Computer Skills Program for teenagers and young people who are unemployed but willing to keep themselves busy. I use the church premises located in the informal settlement of eNkanini in Khayelitsha called Cape Town Fountain of Hope to do my trainings and classes, it is the church where I volunteer as a Project Administrator and a Caregiver.
